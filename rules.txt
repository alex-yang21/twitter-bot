Gungan translator rules:
  1. try to use mappings from https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gungan_Basic
  2. try not to use old Gungan
  3. use our own rules

Our rules:
  1. follow English grammar, just use 1-1 mappings
  2. add 'a' after any word that ends in 's'
  3. add 'sa' after any word that ends in a 'vowel'
  4. add 'en' or 'ing' to verbs but not sure how feasible
  5. don't/do not--> no
  6. expect --> spect
  ...
